The Spoken World is a fictional universe where the spoken word, amplified by song, has the power of creation. The world unknowingly teeters on the brink of conflict, with three unlikely heroes at the center of the struggle. Filled with intrigue and adventure, what is sung in the Spoken World has the power to unite hearts, restore life, and deliver the world from the brink of catastrophe.
